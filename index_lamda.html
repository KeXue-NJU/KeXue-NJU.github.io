    Research Interest

    I am interested in black-box optimization algorithms, such as:
  • Evolutionary algorithms [c2], especially Quality-Diversity algorithms [c3, c7, i1, c11, c13, c14];
  • Bayesian optimization [c1, c4, c10];
  • Learning to optimize [c5, i2, c12, c15, c16];
    • and their applications, such as:
  • Human-AI coordination [c6, c8, j1, j3, j4];
  • Electronic design automation [c9, i3, c17, c18];
  • AI for science [j2];
